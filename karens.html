Karen's Bagels

Project Type: Branding | App Design | Web Design

Brief: Karen's Bagels is a small local business in Liverpool that delivers Bagels to your door. Karen's ideal customer is a single female in their early 20s, who enjoys responsibly sourced, handcrafted items. A strong brand identity was required that incorporates the owners love of 60s diners and icludes the slogan 'freshly baked with love'. A clean app design and mobile-first web option were also needed.

Key Words: Unique | Handcrafted | Delicious | Sustainable | Nostalgic

Comments: The idea for Karen's branding was to create a striking mix of the nostalgic and modern, to evoke feelings of both classic handcrafted items and a responsible, sustainable health conscious company. I wanted the selected typefaces for the brand to feel gritty and imperfect connotating uniqueness. The mix of a more vintage colour palette and modern design aesthetics works well for a brand that wants to catch the attention of a young adult in 2022. The heart I added to the logo ties in with the company slogan and allows for a pop of the pink colour selected in the pallette. The layout and interactivity of both the app and web was kept simple and intuitive, allowing the product and the users to be the focus.
