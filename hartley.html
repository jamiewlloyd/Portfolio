Hartley Farm

Project Type: Logotype | Poster Design | Social Media

Target Persona: Local residents, mainly young families and couples who attend events.

Problem to Solve: Hartley farm needed a logo, poster and set of instagram graphics that could be used to promote their upcoming events (a new financial venture). The logo had to be type-only, simplistic, rustic and modern. The promotional materials had to grab attention while looking classic and autumnal.

Key Words: Farm | Rustic | Modern | Family | Classic

Comments: "The focus creating this logo was finding the correct typeface. While exploring typefaces I concentrated on the key words rustic and modern. The dark dusty blue chosen for the logotype is classic but also elevates the branding. For the promotional materials, a muted colour palette was used, complimenting the blue of the logo and allowing the illustrations to pop and grab attention. Capitalisation was also used sparingly to grab attention."
