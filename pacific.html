Pacific Calm

Project Type: Branding | Logo

Problem to Solve: Pacific Calm is a health club and spa who focus on reducing stress. This focus on the ‘body and soul’ set them apart from local competition. Branding and a logo (with logomark and logotype) was required to rebrand the health club. The aim of the rebrand is to convey an identity which better fits their services.

Key Words: Pacific | Calm | Stress-Free | Health | Soul
