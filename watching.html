They Are Watching

Project Type: Poster | Motion Graphic

Problem to Solve: A personal challenge to create a movie poster for a sci-fi horror movie. I wanted it to feel dark and unsettling and look inspired by old 70s B movies. The poster image itself is a composite of stock photography with adjustment layers to create the effect. The promo motion graphic created to go with the poster to be used for social media.

Key Words: Sci-fi | Horror | Psychological | Old-fashioned | Unsettling
