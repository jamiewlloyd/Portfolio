The Drunken Macaw

Project Type: Branding | Logo | Social Media

Problem to Solve: The Drunken Macaw is an upcoming pirate themed restaurant and bar, Hoping to provide premium themed dining and party experiences. A strong brand identity was requested that matched the pirate theme. The main aim of the brand is to be as shareable on social media as possible. A mascot style logo was also requested as the target audience is both families for the restaurant and young adults for the bar.

Key Words: Pirate | Shareable | Fun | Whimsical | Premium
