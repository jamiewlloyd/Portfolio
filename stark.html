Stark Gyms

Project Type: Poster | Advertising

Problem to Solve: A marketing campaign for a fictional fitness chain 'Stark Gyms'. The aim of the campaign is to drive web traffic and ultimately increase membership signup. I wanted to contrast online culture with old-school print advertising to create posters that grab attention and have the potential to be shared online by people passing by.
